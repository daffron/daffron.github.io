My Thoughts on Time

After reading through the resources on the 'Time' page of the EDA curriculum repository, I have a fair idea of what techniques may and may not work for me;

Things that may work:

  • The Pomodoro Technique - I have had no real chance to actually try this out yet, but it seems like something that would suit my learning style.
  • Growth Mindset - The idea of small task to achieve confidence to accomplish bigger tasks makes alot of sense to me, as I have noticed this without realising in alot of other aspects in life.

Things that may not work:

  • Meditiation - I dont believe I have the right mindset to be able to do this, but who knows, that may change!
  • Mindfulness - Similar to the above point, I dont think I have to abilty to be able to completely clear my mind.

In conclusion I am most interested in the Pomodoro Technique, so will be digger deeper into that first
